How do I track my progress in CrushSmoking?
CrushSmoking automatically tracks your smoke-free days, money saved, and health improvements. Simply open the app daily to log your progress and view your achievements.
What should I do when I have a craving?
Use the "I'm craving the puff" button in the app. It will guide you through a breathing exercise and provide motivational support to help you overcome the craving.
How is my data stored and protected?
Your data is stored locally on your device and is never shared with third parties. We prioritize your privacy and security above all else.
Can I use CrushSmoking on multiple devices?
Currently, CrushSmoking stores your data locally on each device. We're working on cloud sync features for future updates.
Is CrushSmoking free to use?
Yes! CrushSmoking is completely free to use. We believe everyone should have access to tools that help them quit smoking.
